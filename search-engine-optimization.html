Search Engines

To give the best results to user Search Engine looks many factors, they used many algorithms, including the words of your query, relevance and usability of pages, content thats shows on webpage, images, and all other content their relevancy.
Search Engines crawlers crawl your whole webpage and give result to search engine and then search engine indexing your web page.

What is Search Engine Algorithm?

To every search engine, there is a set of rules, a unique formula, from that this search engine determines the accuracy significance of a web page, some search engine have their own set of rules, from this rule they determine its a real web page or spam/fake, These rules are different for each search engine, but some algorithms are common.

Relevancy.

Search Engine algorithms check the relevancy of a web page, when its scanning the webpage looking for keywords, check the webpage has any relevancy has for the particular keywords. The frequency of keywords also is important to relevancy, So use of good keyword for a webpage is also a complex task.

Individual factors

Search Engine algorithms are the individual factors, thats make all search engine different from each other Each search engine have a unique algorithm and give different results.
